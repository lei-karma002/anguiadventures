DREAMWAVE HOTEL POLANGUI

DREAMWAVE HOTEL

Located in Polangui, within 20 miles of Cagsawa Ruins and 23 miles of Mayon Volcano, Dreamwave Hotel Polangui has accommodations with a restaurant and free WiFi as well as free private parking for guests who drive. The hotel has family rooms. At the hotel the rooms have air conditioning and a flat-screen TV Ibalong Centrum for Recreation is 24 miles from Dreamwave Hotel Polangui. The nearest airport is Naga Airport, 29 miles from the accommodation.

LEONOR’S TRANSIENT HOUSE

HOTEL

Leonor's Transient House is located in Polangui, 20 miles from Cagsawa Ruins, 24 miles from Mayon Volcano, and 24 miles from Ibalong Centrum for Recreation.



NUAT THAI FOOT & BODY MASSAGE

FOOT & BODY MASSAGE

NUAT THAI Foot and Body Massage offers authentic and affordable traditional Thai massage and Thai Aroma therapy. Their service provides relief of stress and body aches, relaxation and it also improves blood circulation. It is located in Maharlika Hwy Polangui. They are open from monday until Sunday.
